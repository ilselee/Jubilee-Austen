Hi, I'm an actress, researcher and almost coder.

A little bit about me.

I've always loved performing.
It's true. I studied Drama at the University of Stellenbosch and then went on to work in South Africa's television industry. In 2017, I set sail to the UK to do an MFA in Acting at the Guildford School of Acting. Coming back at the end of 2019, I was ready to write, perform and direct theatre...

...and then The Rona hit. I decided to start to learn coding in order to keep my brain busy and ended up loving it. So, here we are! I'm still learning, but am looking forward to be able to market my various skills.
